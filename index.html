My Projects

BattleLine

BattleLine was a result of Lantern Light Studios' first ever PADD event (month long game jam). I had the wonderful chance to lead a team on a game idea that I came up with. After winning the PADD event, we were awarded the opportunity to put our game on Steam, free of charge. This project helped me with team leadership, communication, my programming skills, and my overall understanding of game design. I also was able to market the game to potential players, which allowed me to network with players and receive constructive feedback on the game's design and mechanics.

  • My own game design
  • Programming in Unity C#
  • Marketing the game to players
  • Leading and organizing tasks for my team

Golden Striker

Created in just 3 days, Golden Striker was a result of a game jam. I wanted to challenge myself to work on this project alone, so I ventured into new territory. I created the art, and programmed the entire game, while finding SFX from free sound websites. This project was a great challenge for me because I had never worked in art before. It gave me a much better understanding of how to create 2d art, and a good opportunity to hone my programming skills. Now, I am continuing to add features and polish the game.

  • My own game design
  • First time making 2d art. Utilized Aseprite
  • Programmed the entire project

About Me

Since a very young age, video games were a major interest to me. As I grew up, I found that coding was a very fun challenge and was something I could strive at. At first, I went to the University of Toledo,majoring in Computer Science, because I thought that I wouldn't have much of a shot in game development. After a year there, I decided to transfer to Ohio University to major in VR and Game Development. By transferring, I have confirmed that my passion is game development. Now, I am graduating in the Fall 2026 semester and am looking for internships for the Summer 2026 semester. While gaining much coding knowledge in college, I also have experience programming at Protec Coating Company, Lantern Light Studios, and for Ohio University on a grant project. I am extremely dedicated and eager to find new projects and work that I can challenge myself, and bring forth value to the company.
